Frederik Ebert

I'm a visiting researcher from TU Munich (TUM) working at Sergey Levine's lab at the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (BAIR) , where I am writing my master thesis in the field of deep reinforcement learning.

My research interests are the development of novel approaches to apply reinforcement learning and deep learning to robotic manipulation and locomotion.

Previously I have worked at the mechatronics institute of the German Aerospace Center (DLR) on the mechanical design an control system of a quadruped robot.
